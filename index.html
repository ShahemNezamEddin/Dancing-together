about-us

Dancing together sees dancing as a passion we pass to our kids,our school offers fun activities for kids to learn how to dance from a young age

The Ballet is a wonderful way for young children to improve flexibility, gain strength, stay fit and have fun. It teaches them to focus and cooperate with others.

Hayley Descavich began her training at Stoneleigh Burnham School in Greenfield, Massachusetts. She graduated from the art School at the University of Hartford, with a Bachelor of Fine Arts, Dance Pedagogy in 2012.

Kelly Hayes has been teaching Ballet and holds a BFA in Dance from the University of Iowa. she like structured classes and Kelly enjoys sharing ballet technique with her students in a healthy, joyful way.

Classical ballet is the traditional, formal styles of ballet that exclusively employ classical ballet technique. It is known for its aesthetics and rigorous technique and precise movements.

Contemporary ballet is a genre of dance that incorporates elements of classical ballet and modern dance. Many of its attributes come from the ideas and innovations of 20th-century modern dance.
